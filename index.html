Architecture

ISHIZENO 8 is designed with a simple principle:
Analog for signal, digital for everything else.

Dynamic Patching

Dynamic patching is enabled by a matrix of 8 or 16 AD75019 analog crosspoint switches. These switches have no gain and a very low 150 Ω on resistance. No more than two switches are ever used on the analog signal path, thereby limiting the overhead resistance to 300 Ω.

Using dynamic patching, any pair of ports of any pair of channels can be patched together, including two ports of the same channel. Furthermore, dynamic patching allows any analog port to be directly patched to any potentiometer. When the module is equipped with 8 switches, up to 16 concurrent patches can be established at a time. With 16 switches, this limit is increased to 32.

Pivot Charts

You can generate charts and maps directly from the pivot, without any complex range selection or column binding. Simply pivot your dataset, select your desired chart, and voilà!

We provide an extensive collection of charts that can be combined into dashboards and infographics. You can also use the standard D3.js library to add your own custom visualizations.

From there, charts and dashboards can be pushed to mobile devices like smartphones and tablets, without having to keep any cube data in memory. And you can export your pivot data to visualization tools like Tableau®.

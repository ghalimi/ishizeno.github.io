Our Story

We are a synthesizer designer based in Palo Alto, California.
We are bringing the best of analog and digital together.
We are about to let your break new sound barriers.

Modular

Build submodules as Arduino shields.
Patch them with Eurorack modules.
8-channel poliphony in 42-HP.

Hybrid

Use digital controls to drive analog signals.
Experiment with digital crosspoint switching.
Create sounds that nobody has ever heard.

Open

Develop user interfaces as web applications.
Create new submodules on a breadboard.
Open design hardware, open source software.

ISHIZENO 8

8 Polyphonic Channels

Each made of 6 analog ports and 4 digital potentiometers.
Each powered by its very own Arduino microcontroller.

Dynamic Patching

Use regular patching cables the old fashioned way.
Or patch analog ports and knob settings digitally.

8 Submodule Bays

Embed up to 8 submodules packaged as Arduino shields.
Oscillators, filters, sequencers, or whatever you fancy.

Wireless User Interface

Use any smartphone, tablet, or PC as user interface.
Connect to your synthesizer with built-in WiFi hotspot.

48 Channel Oscilloscope

Visualize all 48 analog ports simultaneously.
Get the pulse of any submodule without any cable.

Optional Grid Controller

324 illuminated keys sensitive to pressure, tilt, and velocity.
10 knobs and 8 drum pads. Playing and sequencing nirvana.
